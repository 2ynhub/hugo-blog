Modern Family S01E11 영어 표현

1. out of the blue

갑자기, 난데없이.

- Jay: The hell is he doing here?
- Gloria: Jay.
- Jay: No. He was supposed to pick up Manny yesterday and disappointed him again, as usual. Now he just sails in here out of the blue?


2. by all means

물론, 분명히.

- Phil: By all means, Claire, we want you looking your sexiest when the hunky, gay firemen get here!


3. pool

포켓볼(여러 색깔의 공 16개로 하는 당구 비슷한 게임. 흔히 pub이나 bar에서 함).

- Gloria: What’s the matter?
- Jay: He’s playing on my pool table, that’s what.


4. grab[have, take, get, catch, hold] something by the throat

(사태 따위를) 장악하다, 좌지우지하다, 남의 급소를 쥐다.

- Javier: From me, he learns how to be spontaneous, grab life by the throat.
