1. grow a pair

[Verb] (vulgar,idiomatic) To be brave; to show some courage, especially in a situation in which one has so far failed to do so.

- Gideon: You’re a shrink. Can’t you get a copy of the eval and coach her through it?
- Drew: Oh, no, no. I couldn’t. That would be cheating.
- Samantha: Grow a pair, Bambi.

2. take the high road

to behave in a moral way when other people are not behaving morally.

- Drew: Oh– Uh, I think I accidentally liked it.
- Gina: Drew!
- Drew: Maybe it’ll look like you’re taking the high road.

3. come off

to happen as planned, or to succeed.

- Gina: By the way, you’re getting my kidney.
- Drew: What?
- Gina: I passed the psych test. She doesn’t think I’m crazy, but you didn’t come off so good.
