1. too rich for one’s blood

Too expensive or fancy to suit one’s taste or preferences.

- Lily: So the only way you’re allowed to hook up with her is if you marry her.
- Barney: Too rich for my blood.

2. great Scott

An interjection of surprise, amazement, or dismay.

- Barney: You still dating that guy? (놀라는 표정) Great scotch!
