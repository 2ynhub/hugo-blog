B Positive S01E07 영어 표현

English 2021-02-13 101 words One minute
Contents
  • 1. table
  • 2. get under sb’s skin
  • 3. be/get carried away

1. table

to delay discussion of a subject.

- Gina: I can see your lips moving, but I can not hear you talking.
- Drew: Uh… Why don’t we table this for later?

2. get under sb’s skin

to annoy someone.

- Gina: She said my crazy kidney guy left her a snarky-ass message about how great I am that really got under her skin.

3. be/get carried away

to become so excited about something that you do not control what you say or do.

- Drew: I was trying to bring you closer together, but I think I got a little carried away.
